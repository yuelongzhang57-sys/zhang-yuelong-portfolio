# Design

## Theme
3D romantic cool city. The homepage video is the north star: bright blue sky, cloud-white surfaces, pink-violet highlights, sunset-orange accents, soft depth of field, and toy-like city signage.

## Color
Use OKLCH-based tokens with a luminous sky background and darker blue-violet text for contrast.

- Sky: clear blue gradients and soft cyan washes.
- Cloud: warm white and translucent surface layers.
- Bloom: sakura pink and lavender highlights.
- Sunset: restrained orange for active states and important accents.
- Ink: deep blue-violet for body text, navigation, and structural weight.

## Typography
Keep the current sans-serif stack. Use strong, compact section titles; avoid repeated tiny uppercase labels becoming the main visual motif.

## Components
Navigation should feel like a floating city control bar. Project cards should read as city billboards or portfolio portals, with visible labels and clear click affordance. Cards, media containers, panels, and contact blocks share the same cloud-surface material and restrained shadows.

## Layout
Use responsive shells and grids that collapse cleanly at 900px and 640px. Remove fixed desktop minimum widths. Sections should feel connected through shared color, spacing, and surface language.

## Motion
Use gentle reveal, float, highlight, and hover lift. Disable heavy transforms and long transitions for reduced-motion users. Avoid flip interactions that obscure project meaning on touch devices.
