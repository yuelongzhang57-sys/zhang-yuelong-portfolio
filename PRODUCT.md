# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
Recruiters, collaborators, clients, and creative teams reviewing Zhang Yuelong's portfolio to understand his visual design, AIGC content production, content operations, and cross-disciplinary execution ability.

## Product Purpose
This personal website presents a memorable portfolio and directs visitors toward featured projects, capability evidence, and contact channels. Success means the homepage video and the rest of the site feel like one coherent creative world, while the work remains easy to scan and evaluate.

## Brand Personality
Romantic, playful, cinematic, and slightly cool. The portfolio should feel like a bright 3D toy-city commercial world with credible creative discipline underneath.

## Anti-references
Avoid generic AI portfolio templates, scattered pastel cards with no hierarchy, overused glassmorphism, decorative effects that hide the work, and design changes that expand into a full framework migration.

## Design Principles
Show the world before explaining it.
Make project entries feel like clear portfolio gateways, not raw asset piles.
Keep whimsy anchored by deep readable typography and structured spacing.
Use motion for atmosphere and orientation, not as the main content.
Prefer focused improvements over broad rewrites.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
Target readable contrast for body text and navigation, stable mobile layouts from 375px upward, keyboard-visible interactive states, and a usable reduced-motion experience.
